Web Design

Middlesex Water Company

We designed and programmed the updated website for Middlesex Water Company, a large water utility located in central New Jersey with revenue of $90MM and serving over 125,000 customers.

This complex site contains more than 100 pages with links, attachments and forms. It has all the bells and whistles including
online e-commerce, time and stock ticker, interactive Annual Report, webcasts, streaming video, news bulletins, service alerts and complete investor information. With an onboard content management system, the client is able to update information and post late-breaking events in real time.

Despite its complexity, this site loads exceptionally fast.
